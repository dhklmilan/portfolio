Research Experiences

Distribution of Red Panda in Gaurishankar Conservation Area

The Red Panda, a charismatic and endangered species, inhabits the temperate forests of Nepal. This research highlights habitat preferences, threats from habitat fragmentation, and conservation efforts. The study integrates GIS-based mapping and ecological assessments to identify key regions crucial for Red Panda conservation in Gaurishankar Conservation Area (GCA), Nepal.
